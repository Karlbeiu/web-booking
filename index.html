Crafting Moments of Pure Gastronomy

L’Étoile Dorée was founded on the simple philosophy of elevating local ingredients through contemporary French culinary techniques. Under the direction of Executive Chef Jean-Luc Dorée, we have built a sanctuary for those who appreciate precise cooking, balanced flavors, and unmatched hospitality.

Every plate is designed as a canvas, celebrating color, texture, and taste in perfect harmony. We invite you to experience our legendary dining salon and create unforgettable memories around our tables.

Jean-Luc Dorée
